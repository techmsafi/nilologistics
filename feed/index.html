At Nilo Logistics, we want to end that. Here are three non-negotiable steps for a stress-free shipment:

  1. The Right Address: Always double-confirm our China warehouse address before sending it to your supplier.
  2. The Tracking Link: As soon as your supplier ships, get that tracking number and share it with us. We can’t follow up on what we don’t know exists!
  3. The Follow-Up: Make it a habit to check in a few days after your consignment leaves.

A little bit of proactivity ensures your goods don’t get left behind. We’re here to watch your back, but these three steps make us an unbeatable team.
